    About these input limits

    The ranges above come from the official AHA PREVENT reference equations (AHAprevent v1.0.0; Khan et al., Circulation 2024, DOI 10.1161/CIRCULATIONAHA.123.067626). They reflect the data the regression was developed and validated on.

    For values outside these ranges (e.g. SBP > 200, age > 79), the AHA does not endorse using PREVENT — the model can give clinically misleading risk percentages because those values weren't represented in the development cohort. This calculator follows the AHA convention of refusing to compute, matching the behavior of the public AHA PREVENT web tool.

    For patients outside the validated range, use clinical judgment and consider an alternative tool (e.g. the older Pooled Cohort Equations, or specialty-specific guidelines).
