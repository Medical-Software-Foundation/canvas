# CCP005v1 - Diagnosis Of Asthma

### Target Population
All patients who have an active asthma condition on file.

### Recommendations
For patients with an active asthma diagnosis the protocol surfaces a Due
protocol card showing the date of the earliest onset and recommends scheduling
contact with the patient. Patients without an active asthma diagnosis receive a
Satisfied card.

### Importance
Surfacing an active asthma diagnosis at the chart level helps care teams
proactively reach patients who may benefit from follow-up, medication review or
education on managing their condition.
