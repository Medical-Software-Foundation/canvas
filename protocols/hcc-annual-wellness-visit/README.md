# HCC Annual Wellness Visit

## Target population

Patients aged 65 or older who are due for an Annual Wellness Visit (AWV).

## Recommendations

For patients in the target population who have not had a qualifying AWV CPT
(`G0438`, `G0439`, `G0402`, `99387`, `99397`) billed within the past year, the
protocol surfaces a "due" card recommending that an AWV be scheduled. Patients
with a recent qualifying visit see a "satisfied" card.

## Importance

AWVs play a key role in preventive care for older adults, helping detect
potential health issues early and supporting care planning and chronic
condition management.
