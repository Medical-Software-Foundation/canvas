# HCC002v2 - CKD Suspect

### Target Population
All patients with evidence of two or more elevated eGFR values (< 60 ml/min) over the last two years and no active CKD problem on the Conditions List.

### Recommendations
For any patient with at least two creatinine lab values whose computed eGFR is below 60 ml/min within the last two years, the protocol surfaces a "Diagnose" recommendation on a protocol card, suggesting the conditions list be updated to include kidney-related problems as clinically appropriate.

When the patient already has an active kidney-failure or hypertensive-CKD condition on file, the protocol card is marked satisfied.

### Importance
This protocol provides a continuous, patient-level signal that renal disease may be present and undocumented, making it easy to drive downstream diagnosis and care-coordination workflows.
